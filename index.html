What is dorkbot?

Dorkbot Brighton is a free, regular, informal, show-and-tell style meetup of designers, geeks, artists, engineers, students and anyone else who's interested. It's a place for people to show off the strange things they've been doing with electricity, be it a new gadget, a smartphone app, a website, a synthesiser or an artwork. The Brighton Dorkbot is just one of many all over the world. To find one closer to you check out the main Dorkbot site.

Each meetup consists of several pre-organised speakers/presenters showing off their amazing things & answer any questions. Then we hang out, socialise, network and natter for a bit.
